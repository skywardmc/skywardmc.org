- [Cosmonautic](https://www.youtube.com/@Cosmxnautic) mentioned Adrenaline and Additive in this [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rq8Md9_jLIA) and a [short](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/f3t-JYKwkTc)
